pronouns - Notepad--
my primary set of pronouns are *e/h*/h*/h*s/h*self. they really only work when used in text-based communication. I found them online, they were made as a half-joke for some 90s chat software. they're also called splat pronouns, a name much easier to look up. feel free to replace the asterisks with whatever other unicode symbols work for the platform/you think are cool, although try to avoid emoji or anything else that would be rendered in color. a few that I think would be cool include ꙮ, ‽, or ⌂. get creative!
