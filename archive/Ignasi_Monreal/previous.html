ABOUT

Ignasi Monreal is an artist from Barcelona working in painting, digital art, scenography, creative direction, animation and film.

Known for his trompe l’oeil paintings of dirty dishes or Plats Bruts and his celebrated collaborations with brands such as Gucci, Bulgari, Cartier, Mercedes-Benz, Ruinart, Airbnb, Meta and Zara among others, his work also achieved mainstream exposure through his collaborations in the music industry with artists like Dua Lipa, Rosalia or FKA Twigs and in recent years with the creation of an original scenographies for Swan Lake and La Bayadère at Teatro dell’Opera di Roma.

In addition to his collaborations, Ignasi has exhibited his work worldwide with murals from New York to Shanghai and also with exhibitions like 'Plats Bruts' at La Fresh Gallery in Madrid and 'Mi manchi come il WiFi' at Studio Stefania Miscetti in Rome. He has also participated in group exhibitions, such as 'The Milky Way' at Franco Noero Gallery in Turin and 'Transatlantico' at Mana Contemporary in New Jersey. In 2020, Ignasi created his first public piece, 'Habemus WiFi', for the historic Edificio Plaza España in Madrid, and in 2022, he presented a new series of Plats Bruts with a new solo exhibition named ‘Sobremesa' at Palazzo Monti in Brescia, which was recently restaged –partly– at ARCO Madrid 2023 in collaboration with Ruinart.
