Windo 8 Updates & Announcements

UPDATE 08/08/24: This is the first time in 2 years since Windo 8 has been updated. Fixes and changes include:
- Fixed the window maximization cutoff issue (I THINK.... I need to test on various screen sizes)
- Changing Fonts and UI colors to bring about a slightly more appealing user interface.
- Adding a Taskbar clock (so you do not have declutter your desktop to wait ~30s to see what the time is)
- You can hide taskbar app name clutter, if you have multiple apps open and the taskbar grows to take up space, click the square button on the left of the time to hide currently open apps on the taskbar.
- Fixed launching LCTapps labyrinth from the start menu (it automatically closes upon app selection)
- Various fixes for app window sizing (slightly more stable)
Check additional info!!!
