Hi! My name is

I specialise in building iOS and web applications. I began my programming journey in 2018 when I taught myself Java. By 2021, my self-taught skills led to a full-time paid job as a software developer.

Programming has been a passion of mine since I started, as it provides constant learning opportunities and challenges that allow me to constantly grow and improve. I find it to be a form of self-discovery, as I navigate different obstacles and come up with solutions that both humans and machines can understand.
