We Won a Webby!

We won a Webby for “SURV1VAL” a music promotion/marketing campaign built for Mike Shinoda in partnership with Warner Records!

The campaign combines web2, web3 theory, and AI technology in order to drive revenue, data, and retargeting through an online game while also aimed to enhance interaction within the Shinoda community and increase music consumption across various streaming platforms.
