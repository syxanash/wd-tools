Profile

Erd Yakingun

Product Designer & Maker

Hello dear visitor! Welcome to erdOS, otherwise known as my slice of the internet ...or operating system? I am a product designer, maker, general code abuser from London currently living in Berlin.

Having worked in agencies and start ups, my expertise lay primarily with UI design and building systems around UI. That and rapid prototyping! My experience also stems from building everything from large scale products to the odd side project.

I like to work with ambitious projects and help great people get their ideas off the ground giving them that early boost they need in their ventures journey.

In the past I've had the opportunity to work in various spaces such as food, education and sports. On top of this, I've also spent time building and learning from my own products such as wavey.tips, hotsauce.world and Stadialist.

Whatever your field, if you have an inspiring or fun idea, I'd like to work with you!
