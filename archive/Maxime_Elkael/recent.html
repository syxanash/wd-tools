Research activities

I currently am a PhD student in the AIDY-F2N team at Telecom Sud-Paris.
My main activities are to
  • Work on network slicing and virtualisation
  • Use artificial intelligence techniques (deep learning, mainly with PyTorch and reinforcement learning) in order to achieve better resource utilization in virtualized networks.
  • Develop a Python library for simulating such networks and testing our approaches.
The goal is to enable various usecases such as autonomous vehicles, VR and sensor networks to use the same network. They should share resources seemlessly and elastically, similarly to what is done with cloud computing
