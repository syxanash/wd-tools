About me

Got my first job as Web Designer in 2007, working with HTML, CSS, Photoshop and Flash, and since then I've accumulated hands-on experience in both web/UI/UX design and frontend development.

I have 15 years of HTML and CSS experience, as well as 9 years of SASS/SCSS and WordPress experience. I also work with accessibility (WCAG), web performance (Performance Insights, Lighthouse), and UX (including software like Adobe XD), Photoshop (since 2007), Illustrator (since 2016), Adobe XD (since 2019), Sketch and Figma.

What I currently do

  2. SASS/SCSS, CSS3, HTML5, frontend
  3. WordPress theme development
  4. On-page SEO, schema.org JSON-LD and structured data markup
  5. Performance optimisation using Lighthouse and Performance Insights
